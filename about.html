THE MOVEMENT
AGAINST FOOD WASTE
Second Chance Foods was founded by college students
to fight food waste. After one of our founder members
worked at our university dining hall. He realized that
so much food was going uneaten and thrown daily. But
what if there was a way to salvage that food and use it
or give it to those incapable of getting a meal.

Founded in 2022, Second Chance Foods made it a life
mission to help reduce food waste and feed our local
communities. As a nonprofit organization, we are proud
to partenr up with many local restaurants and schools
to reporpouse their meals, in order to give back to those
that might go hungry.
